Frequently Asked Questions

1. What cleaning and maintenance services do you offer? +
We specialize in a wide range of services, including roof cleaning, mold removal, floor polishing, and carpet cleaning. Our team is dedicated to keeping your space clean, fresh, and looking brand new!
2. Are your cleaning products safe for my home and family? +
Absolutely! We prioritize your health and safety. Our cleaning products are eco-friendly and non-toxic, making them safe for your home, pets, and family. You can relax knowing we use only the best for your space.
3. How do I schedule a cleaning service? +
Scheduling a service with us is simple! Just click the "Book Now" button on our website, choose the service you need, and pick a time that works best for you. We'll handle the rest to ensure a seamless experience.
4. Will roof cleaning damage my shingles? +
Not at all! Our roof cleaning experts use safe, gentle methods that effectively remove dirt, moss, and algae without causing any damage to your shingles. Your roof will look brand new while maintaining its integrity.
5. How often should I get my carpets cleaned? +
Carpet cleaning depends on the foot traffic and use. However, we recommend a deep cleaning every 6-12 months to maintain cleanliness, remove allergens, and prolong the life of your carpet. For high-traffic areas, more frequent cleanings may be necessary.
6. What causes mold, and how can you help? +
Mold thrives in damp, dark areas, often in basements, bathrooms, or kitchens. If left untreated, it can damage your property and affect your health. Our mold removal experts use safe techniques to remove mold and prevent future growth, ensuring a healthier home.
